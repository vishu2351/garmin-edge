How to Install the Garmin Edge Update

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Charge Your Edge: Ensure the battery is above 50% or plugged in.
  2. Open Garmin Express: Connect your device to a computer with a USB cable.
  3. Sync Your Device: Garmin Express will detect available updates.
  4. Install Update: Click “Install” and wait for the process to complete.
  5. Restart & Sync: Reboot the Edge and sync with Garmin Connect app to finalize.

Alternative: For Wi-Fi-enabled Edge models, go to Menu > Settings > System > Software Update.
